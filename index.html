About 30 Days of Google Cloud

30 Days of Google Cloud program will provide you an opportunity to kickstart your career in cloud and get hands-on practice on Google Cloud Platform - the tool that powers apps like Google Search, Gmail and YouTube.









We have two exciting tracks for you :

Cloud Engineering

List of quest to be completed

  • Getting Started: Create and Manage Cloud Resources

  • Perform Foundational Infrastructure Tasks in Google Cloud

  • Setup and Configure a Cloud Environment in Google Cloud

  • Deploy and Manage Cloud Environments with Google Cloud

  • Build and Secure Networks in Google Cloud

  • Deploy to Kubernetes in Google Cloud

  • Join Whatsapp Group for Cloud Engineering Track

Data Science & Machine Learning

List of quest to be completed

  • Getting Started: Create and Manage Cloud Resources

  • Perform Foundational Data, ML, and AI Tasks in Google Cloud

  • Insights from Data with BigQuery

  • Engineer Data in Google Cloud

  • Integrate with Machine Learning APIs

  • Explore Machine Learning Models with Explainable AI

  • Join Whatsapp Group for Data Science & Machine Learning Track

FAQ's



What is 30 Days of Google Cloud?
30 Days of Google Cloud program will provide you an opportunity to kickstart your career in cloud and get hands-on practice on Google Cloud Platform - the tool that powers apps like Google Search, Gmail and YouTube.
What is Qwiklabs?
Qwiklabs is a platform where you can learn various Cloud technologies on GCP and AWS.
Do I need to pay or buy anything?
No, the course is free and you are provided with 600 credits to complete our tracks in Qwiklabs.
I completed one track, what should I do?
You may or may not learn another track (Up to you).
I have completed the required tracks and/or skill badges. When will I get my prizes?
If you have completed any of the tracks mentioned in the Prize Rules section and are eligible for prizes, then you will get them delivered to you after the program is finished.
What are other benefits I get?
Upon completing each quest you get a digital badge, certifying you that you are proficient in that domain of GCP.
I have already completed the skill badges in the program, what should I do?
Please note that in order to get the prizes, you need to complete the skill badges after 26th September 2020 and before 5th November 2020 11.59 PM IST. Any quests completed before that won't be counted.
Is there any specific day by which I need to complete the quests?
Yes, you can complete your quests anytime before 31st October.
I have already completed one lab on ML, do I need to do it again?
No, if you have already completed one or more lab continue doing the remaining quests.
